Terms

Here are the work conditions and my steps to approach every project / order.

01. Agreement

This concludes your problem and waht we can offer to derive the solution. Work hours & billing is discussed at this step.

02. Prepayment

Working on the project will start after recieving 50% prepayment. You can send it via direct Bank transfer or Wise Account.

03. Filling out the brief

After filling out some questions, we'll be able to find your exact need. Fill out the brief here.

04. Concepts development

Based on your brief, we'll work on developing concenpts through communication and turning out the best possibilities.

05. Refinement and Approval

At this step, We'll work to perfectionize every aspect & make designs ready for the real world out there. This also include revisions and finalizing.

06. Postpayment

After the approval of final product, You'll have to make the final payment. All the files and contents will be delivered in secure medium.
